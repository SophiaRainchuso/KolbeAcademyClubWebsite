The Kolbe Academy Club Hub

Take a look at all of the amazing clubs Kolbe has to offer!

How to Join

Club registration is always open! To register, go to the Parent Portal, click on your student’s name, then the “Registrations” tab where you’ll find a link to “Join a club.” Activity clubs have a small fee associated. Clubs are typically launched in early September. Watch the Kolbe memo for details. Clubs are made by staff and students alike. If you would like to start a new club, please contact your guidance counselor or your teacher. Please keep in mind that starting a club takes marturity, leadership, time, and effort!

Interested in the National Honors Society?

The Kolbe Academy chapter of the National Honor Society is an organization for students who demonstrate a commitment to excellence in academics and service. Qualified students and parents are notified in August when applications for new membership opens.

What We Do

Members Commitments: • $20 NHS annual dues • A minimum of 12 service points per semester. • Qualifying hours can come from the following: ⚬ Providing peer tutoring at Kolbe ⚬ Leadership or service roles in a Kolbe club or group. ⚬ Additional service opportunities provided directly by Kolbe’s Chapter of NHS and some outside service. ⚬ Regular participation on NHS’s Schoology page. ⚬ Mandatory monthly meetings. Days/times vary based on student schedules

Facilitator

Questions? Contact Mrs. Prudlo at tprudlo@kolbe.org or Mrs. Mammola at jmammola@kolbe.org for more information!
